About Kimberly

Kimberly is an organizational communication student who hopes to use her degree to eventually be an event planner. She has experience in event planning from sources she has taken at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire and the University of Winchester, UK. She has experience with event planning through her time as a celebratin event decorator at Community Benefit Tree in Kaukauna, WI. This summer, she will be interning at the Experimental Aircraft Association as an event planning intern. When she isn't planning events, Kimberly works as a writing assistant at UW-Eau Claire's Center for Writing Excellence.
